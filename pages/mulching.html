Mulching is the practice of covering the soil with organic or inorganic materials such as straw, plastic sheets, dry leaves, or crop residues. It helps conserve soil moisture, reduce weed growth, maintain soil temperature, and improve soil fertility.

  • Reduce evaporation and conserve soil moisture
  • Suppress weed growth
  • Improve soil structure and fertility
  • Protect soil from erosion
  • Regulate soil temperature for better plant growth
