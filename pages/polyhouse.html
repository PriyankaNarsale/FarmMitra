Polyhouse cultivation is a modern technique of protected farming using a structure covered with transparent polyethylene sheets. It allows control of temperature, humidity, and light, resulting in higher yield and off-season crop production.

Ideal for: Tomatoes, Capsicum, Cucumbers, Strawberries, Flowers, Medicinal plants.

üèó Polyhouse Structure

  • Frame: Galvanized steel or aluminum
  • Cover: UV-stabilized polyethylene film (single/double layer)
  • Ventilation: Ridge vents, side vents, exhaust fans
  • Irrigation & Fertigation: Drip or sprinkler systems
  • Climate sensors: Temperature, humidity, light, and CO‚ÇÇ monitoring

üíß Irrigation & Fertilization
