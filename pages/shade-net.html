Shade net cultivation uses a structure covered with shade nets to protect crops from excessive sunlight, wind, and pests. It reduces stress on plants, improves water efficiency, and increases yield quality.

Ideal for: Leafy vegetables, herbs, nursery plants, flowers, and ornamental crops.

  • Support structures for hanging crops like tomato or cucumber
