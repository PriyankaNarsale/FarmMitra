Green manuring is a traditional and eco-friendly practice where **green plants are grown and plowed back into the soil** to increase its fertility, structure, and organic matter. It improves soil health naturally without synthetic fertilizers.

Best For: Organic farming, soil improvement, sustainable agriculture.

üéØ Objectives

  • Increase nitrogen and organic matter in the soil
  • Improve soil structure and water-holding capacity
  • Reduce soil erosion
  • Suppress weeds naturally
  • Enhance microbial activity and soil biodiversity

üåø How Green Manuring Works

Leguminous crops fix atmospheric nitrogen and return nutrients to the soil when incorporated.

  • Plants grow rapidly and produce biomass
  • Biomass is plowed into the field at flowering stage
  • Organic matter decomposes and enriches the soil
  • Releases nutrients gradually for the next crop
