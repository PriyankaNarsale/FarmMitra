Overview

Sheep farming is profitable due to low feed cost, high adaptability, and consistent meat & wool demand. They thrive in drought areas and grow well with pasture-based systems.

Popular Sheep Breeds in India

Nali

Excellent wool; found in Rajasthan.

Deccani

Hardy breed; ideal for Maharashtra & Karnataka.

Gurez

High altitude breed; strong & disease-resistant.

Mandya

Best for meat; fast-growing lambs.

Feeding Routine

Sheep Shed Design

  • Raised floor for moisture control
  • Open grazing space required
  • Proper fencing to avoid predators
  • Shade & dry area for lambs

Common Sheep Diseases

Foot Rot

Wet conditions cause hoof infection.

PPR

Highly infectious; vaccination required.

Enterotoxaemia

Sudden death; vaccinate regularly.

Worms & Parasites

Common in grazing animals; deworming is essential.

Sheep Care Tips

  • Rotate grazing areas
  • Regular hoof trimming
  • Keep bedding dry
  • Deworm every 3 months
