Greenhouse cultivation involves growing crops in controlled conditions. It protects crops from extreme weather and pests while allowing year-round production of high-value vegetables, fruits, flowers, and herbs.

Ideal for: Tomatoes, Capsicum, Cucumbers, Strawberries, Roses.

  • Accessories: Fans, vents, drip irrigation, heating/cooling systems
  • Sensors: Temperature, humidity, and light control
