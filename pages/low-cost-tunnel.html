Low-cost tunnel cultivation is an affordable method of protected farming using simple polyethylene-covered tunnels. It protects crops from extreme weather, pests, and helps in off-season production.

Ideal for: Tomatoes, capsicum, leafy vegetables, nursery crops, and flowers.

üèó Tunnel Structure

  • Frame: Bamboo, PVC, or galvanized steel
  • Cover: Transparent polyethylene sheet
  • Shape: Semi-circular or ridge type
  • Ventilation: Side flaps or roll-up ends
  • Irrigation: Drip or sprinkler system
