Overview

Rabbit farming is gaining popularity due to cheap input cost, fast multiplication, and high-quality meat. Rabbits are calm animals and suitable for small farms, households, and commercial units.

Rabbit Housing

  • Use cages or hutches
  • Proper ventilation and shade
  • Keep cages dry and clean
  • Wire-mesh floors help maintain hygiene
  • Protect from predators

Common Rabbit Diseases

Coccidiosis

Diarrhea; requires anticoccidial treatment.

Snuffles

Cold & nasal discharge; maintain hygiene.

Ear Mites

Scratching & irritation; apply ear drops.

Enteritis

Digestive upset; avoid sudden feed changes.

Care Tips

  • Provide fresh water 24/7
  • Clean cages regularly
  • Trim nails monthly
