Overview

Pigs grow fast, reproduce quickly, and require minimal space, making pig farming ideal for commercial and small farmers. Proper breed selection, feed, and hygiene are key to better growth and profit.

Housing & Pen Design

  • Proper drainage flooring
  • Separate pens for growers & sows
  • Temperature control (heat sensitive)
  • Clean drinking water 24/7
  • Regular cleaning to prevent disease

Common Pig Diseases

Swine Fever

High fever; immediate vet care required.

Foot & Mouth Disease

Mouth sores & drooling; vaccinate yearly.

Worm Infestation

Weight loss; regular deworming needed.

Pneumonia

Breathing issues; keep pens dry.
